About

SmartTuberculosis is a webapp that is used for diagnosis of Tuberculosis- using Image Processing and Deep Learning. The detection happens in two phases.In the first phase, the Xray Image is preprocessed with Binary & Otsu Thresholding with Histogram Equalization. The second phase consists of the prediction of Tuberculosis- on the pretrained model
